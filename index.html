So this is the site I plan on using and distributing to allow anyone to easily find information about the big three role playing games: Pathfinder, D&D 3.5 and D&D 5. The newest edition is 5th edition. 3.5 is not being updated anymore, so it's data will most likely not change unless a website goes down. Pathfinder is still updating and is considered by some to be a more balanced version of 3.5 and is in fact built off the 3.5 sytem. Most of it's data is open-source whereas the other systems are not. If you are wondering why I am not including 4th edition, it's because most people generally considered it rather bad compared to 3.5 due to how they drastically changed how casting and abilities worked. It was just percieved as a generally bad system to the old fans even though it made getting into the system super easy.
